About Back of the Envelope

This publication is written to share independent research on Indian markets, macro trends, commodities, and sector-level analysis — with the goal of education, peer review, and open discussion.

The name comes from the idea of "back-of-the-envelope" calculations — the kind of first-principles reasoning that cuts through noise to find what actually matters. Each piece tries to identify the one or two variables that drive an outcome, rather than cataloguing everything that might.

For real-time commentary and updates, follow on X (@BackotEnvelope). For deeper discussions on specific pieces, each article links to a dedicated GitHub Discussions thread.
